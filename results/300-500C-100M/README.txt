============================================================
MC SIMULATION CONFIGURATION 1
============================================================

PROJECT INTENT:
This simulation serves as a long run to test the evolution 
of the polymer under a stable temperature (300K). The initial 
configuration was a straight chain (conf_type = 1).

SYSTEM PARAMETERS:
- Atoms: 500 Carbon chain 
- Total Steps: 100.000.000 (10^8)
- Max Rotation (delta): 0.20 radians

ANNEALING PROTOCOL:
- Temperature = 300 K
No annealing process.

KEY RESULTS:
- Integration Status: SUCCESSful. 
- The polymer evolved from a straight chain to a complex
  structure with several compact folds of straight segments 
  and a big loop.
- Total Energy oscillated between 50 and 100 kcal/mol.
- Radius of Gyration stabilized around 50-60 Angstroms.
- End-to-End distance stabilized around 0-30 Angstroms.
- Torsion angles distributed mostly around 180 degrees with
  some values around 60 (and -60) degrees.
